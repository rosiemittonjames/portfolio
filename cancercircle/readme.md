# Cancer Circle 
### As my final year project at the University of Leeds, I created Cancer Circle. 

Cancer Circle is a purpose-driven project which aims to create an inclusive and accessible app which helps reduce the feeling of loneliness for those who have been affected by cancer in the Yorkshire community. This is by connecting users through similarities that are important to them. Examples of these similarities are types of cancer, cancer experiences, hobbies, culture etc as not everyone wants to connect over their type of cancer. 

Networking was a key part of this project. Dr Stewart Manning, a qualified doctor who is now the chairman of the Yorkshire Cancer Community, mentored me throughout my project. As a result of this, I went to three Patient Panels (user feedback sessions) where I spoke to NHS employees, Macmillan employees, cancer charity CEOs, and cancer patients themselves. 

### Process 
Research: 
- Speaking to professionals and researchers
- Speaking to cancer patients
- Attending patient panels and pitching my ideas, designing, and testing the UI and UX design

Design
- Wireframing
- Designed the logo
- Created a colour palette
- Designed the whole platform

Coding 
- I used languages: HTML, CSS, JavaScript, and PHP
- I also used MySQL to collect user data

### Result 
- I got 79/100 in my project
- 100% of people asked would have used Cancer Circle in their journey
- 100% of people thought Cancer Circle was a good idea
- Cancer Circle’s website abides by the Web Content Accessibility Guidelines - there's only 1 error that doesn’t align with the guidelines
